# 🎥 Demo Video Script - World Cup Fan Intelligence Hub

**Duration**: 3 minutes  
**Target**: IBM SkillsBuild AI Builders Challenge Judges  
**Format**: Screen recording with voiceover

---

## 📋 Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Start Streamlit application (`streamlit run app/main.py`)
- [ ] Clear browser cache for clean demo
- [ ] Prepare screen recording software (OBS Studio, Loom, or Zoom)
- [ ] Test microphone audio quality
- [ ] Close unnecessary browser tabs and applications
- [ ] Set screen resolution to 1920x1080 for best quality
- [ ] Have example teams ready: Brazil, Argentina, Germany, France

---

## 🎬 Video Structure

### Opening (0:00 - 0:30) - 30 seconds

**Visual**: Landing page of the application

**Script**:
> "Hi! I'm [Your Name], and this is the World Cup Fan Intelligence Hub - my submission for the IBM SkillsBuild AI Builders Challenge.
>
> The problem? Football fans struggle to predict match outcomes and understand the reasoning behind predictions. Traditional analytics are too complex for casual fans.
>
> My solution combines machine learning with IBM watsonx.ai to deliver instant predictions with plain-English explanations that anyone can understand."

**Actions**:
- Show landing page with IBM branding
- Highlight the three main features
- Point to dataset statistics (49,329 matches, 154 years)

---

### Feature 1: Match Predictor (0:30 - 1:30) - 60 seconds

**Visual**: Navigate to Match Predictor page

**Script**:
> "Let me show you the Match Predictor. I'll predict a classic rivalry: Brazil versus Argentina.
>
> [Select teams] I choose Brazil as home team, Argentina as away team, set the date to June 30th, 2026, and mark it as a neutral venue since it's a World Cup match.
>
> [Click Predict] Our RandomForest model, trained on 25,000 historical matches, predicts... [wait for result] a Draw with 82% confidence!
>
> But here's the magic - IBM watsonx.ai's Llama 3.3 70B model explains WHY in plain English. [Read excerpt of explanation]
>
> You can see the probability distribution, key features like team form and head-to-head record, and even download a detailed report."

**Actions**:
- Select Brazil and Argentina
- Set date and neutral venue
- Click "Predict Match Outcome"
- Wait for prediction to load (show loading spinner)
- Highlight the prediction result
- Scroll to show AI explanation
- Point to probability chart
- Show key features section
- Briefly show download button

---

### Feature 2: Team Statistics (1:30 - 2:15) - 45 seconds

**Visual**: Navigate to Team Statistics page

**Script**:
> "Next, the Team Statistics dashboard. Let's analyze Brazil's complete history.
>
> [Select Brazil and click Analyze] We see Brazil has played over 1,000 matches with a 65% win rate. The dashboard shows their overall record, goals statistics, and home versus away performance.
>
> [Scroll down] Here's their recent form - the last 10 matches with results. And this chart shows their win rate trend over the decades.
>
> All visualizations are interactive, and you can download comprehensive reports for any team."

**Actions**:
- Navigate to Team Statistics page
- Select Brazil from dropdown
- Click "Analyze Team"
- Show key metrics cards
- Point to pie chart and bar chart
- Scroll to recent form section
- Show performance trends chart
- Briefly mention download feature

---

### Feature 3: Head-to-Head Analysis (2:15 - 3:00) - 45 seconds

**Visual**: Navigate to Head-to-Head page

**Script**:
> "Finally, Head-to-Head Analysis. Let's compare Brazil and Argentina's rivalry.
>
> [Select teams and analyze] They've played 115 times since 1914! Brazil leads with 47 wins to Argentina's 42, with 26 draws.
>
> [Scroll] The timeline shows results over time, and here's the complete match history with scores, venues, and tournaments.
>
> This is powered by 154 years of football data - from 1872 to 2026 - making it the most comprehensive football analytics tool for fans.
>
> The entire project is open source on GitHub, uses IBM watsonx.ai for AI explanations, and is ready for the world to use. Thank you!"

**Actions**:
- Navigate to Head-to-Head page
- Select Brazil and Argentina
- Click "Analyze Head-to-Head"
- Show overall record card
- Point to win/draw/loss statistics
- Scroll to timeline visualization
- Show match history table
- End on a positive note with GitHub mention

## 🎤 Voiceover Tips

- **Pace**: Speak clearly and not too fast (aim for 150-160 words per minute)
- **Energy**: Be enthusiastic but professional
- **Pauses**: Brief pauses after key points for emphasis
- **Clarity**: Pronounce technical terms clearly (watsonx.ai, RandomForest, etc.)
- **Confidence**: You built something amazing - show it!

---

## 🎨 Visual Tips

- **Smooth Transitions**: Use fade or slide transitions between pages
- **Cursor Highlighting**: Use a cursor highlighter tool if available
- **Zoom In**: Zoom in on important elements (predictions, charts)
- **No Errors**: Test everything before recording to avoid errors on camera
- **Loading States**: If something takes time to load, acknowledge it naturally
